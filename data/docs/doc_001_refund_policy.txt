ACME REFUND POLICY v1.2
Effective date: 2025-09-01

1. Eligibility
- Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds are not available for services that have been fully used.

2. Refund Amount
- If a refund is approved within 7 days of purchase, customers receive 100%.
- If approved between 8–30 days, customers receive 70%.
- After 30 days, refunds are denied.

3. Exceptions
- Annual plans are refundable only if no more than 2 support tickets were opened.
- Promotional purchases marked FINAL SALE are not refundable.

4. Processing Time
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after approval.
