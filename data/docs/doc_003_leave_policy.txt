Vacation
- Full-time employees accrue 1.25 vacation days per month (15 days/year).
- Unused vacation may carry over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.

Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 sick days per year.
- Sick leave does not carry over.

Approval
- Vacation requests longer than 5 consecutive business days require manager approval at least 2 weeks in advance.
- Emergency leave may be requested with same-day notice.

Holidays
- The company observes 11 paid holidays.
