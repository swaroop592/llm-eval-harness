Access Control
- Production access requires MFA and is granted on a least-privilege basis.
- Privileged access is reviewed quarterly.

Data Handling
- Customer PII must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Logs containing PII must be retained for no more than 30 days.

Incident Reporting
- Security incidents must be reported within 30 minutes of detection.
- A post-incident review must be completed within 5 business days.

Passwords
- Minimum length: 12 characters.
- Must include upper, lower, number, and symbol.
- Password reuse is prohibited for the last 5 passwords.
