SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) — ACME CLOUD
Revision: 3.0

Availability
- Monthly uptime target: 99.9%
- Uptime excludes scheduled maintenance and force majeure events.

Maintenance
- Scheduled maintenance occurs on Sundays 01:00–03:00 UTC.
- Customers are notified at least 48 hours in advance.

Service Credits
- If uptime < 99.9% and >= 99.0%: 10% monthly credit
- If uptime < 99.0% and >= 98.0%: 25% monthly credit
- If uptime < 98.0%: 50% monthly credit

Claim Window
- Credit claims must be submitted within 14 days of the end of the affected month.
- Claims must include incident timestamps and affected region.

Support Response
- Severity 1: response within 1 hour
- Severity 2: response within 4 hours
- Severity 3: response within 1 business day
